WHY DRIP CAMPAIGNS?

Drip e-mail campaigns are part of marketing automation tools.

Campaign usually consists of series of emails which are scheduled with aim to move customer down the sales funnel. The main goal is to build build relationship gradually and not trying to put every piece of content in one hard sales email.. Because campaign consists of set of emails it is possible to adapt content on the way. For example based on customer click history or purchase behaviour.

As a result customers have personalised experience and store owners have boosted revenue and increased customer loyalty!
